Peacock

Peacocks are the most beautiful birds that live on the surface of the globe, and is sometimes called the King of birds because of its attractive and wonderful colors.

  • Scientific Name: Pavo
  • Average Length: About 90-130 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 10-25 years
  • Habital: Forests of tropical countries in Asia

The peacock's most famous bird is its colored feathers. The peacock has a large fan behind it, about five times the length of its body, and is seen in front of people. It is usually known for beauty, pride, arrogance and vanity.
